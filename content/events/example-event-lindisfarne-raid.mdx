The raid on Lindisfarne in 793 is traditionally considered the beginning of the Viking Age.

Norse raiders attacked the monastery on the island of Lindisfarne off the northeast coast of England. The event shocked the Christian world and is often cited by historians as the symbolic beginning of the Viking Age.
